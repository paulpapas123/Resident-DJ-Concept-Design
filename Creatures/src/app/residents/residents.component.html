Some Sheila

Role: Resident DJ

Grab your tequila, prepare for a squealer, we're introducing Some Sheila. She's more than the sheila that DJ’d some house party on the weekend, more than the DJ that has just one sound and more than a one spin wonder. She's the sheila you wait for, the sheila that doesn't roll her eyes at a song request and the sheila that understands her crowd. She's Some Sheila. Despite the name, Some Sheila isn’t just any sheila. Some Sheila has taken the stage across Europe, including rooftops in London and beach clubs and in Santorini, as well as dominating her stomping ground across Melbourne. Some Sheila is a Resident DJ for Creatures Events and has supported other well-known Melbourne collectives, such as Pounce Events, The Daily Commute and President Press. Her music is not limited to a specific genre. Instead her tastes lie with beats that keep you moving, lyrics you can’t get out of your head and songs that bring an element of surprise to the dance floor. Why Some Sheila? Because she's loud, she's proud and wins over the god damn crowd.

Platforms: Facebook DJ Soundcloud

G Man

Role: Resident DJ

G Man is Melbourne based, born and raised DJ and producer. A founding member of Creatures, G Man has been honing his DJing style clubs around Melbourne since 2014. With a focus on Tech house and UK bass house, G Man loves to inject colour into the stereotypical club scene. You can expect the dance floor to be hit with left of centre bangers which you will find impossible to not groove to. G Man is also an up and coming producer in the Aussie trap scene. Having recently released his debut EP, we are keen for G Man to bring his taste and supreme knowledge to techno and house.

Platform: Facebook DJ Soundcloud Producer
